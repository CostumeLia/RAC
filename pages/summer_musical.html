Alice in Wonderland - Summer 2025

Alice, a restless young girl, follows a White Rabbit down a rabbit hole and tumbles into a bizarre world called Wonderland. She encounters a series of strange and illogical characters, including a Cheshire Cat with a vanishing grin, a Mad Hatter and March Hare holding a never-ending tea party, and a tyrannical Queen of Hearts obsessed with beheading people. Through her adventures, Alice learns to embrace the power of her imagination. She awakens back in her own world, inspired by her journey and ready to approach life with a newfound sense of wonder and the courage to be herself.
